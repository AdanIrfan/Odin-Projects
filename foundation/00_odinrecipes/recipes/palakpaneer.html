Palak Paneer Recipe

North Indian Style

Palak Paneer is a creamy spinach curry cooked with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), flavored with aromatic spices. Fresh spinach is blanched and blended into a smooth puree, then cooked with onions, tomatoes, garlic, and traditional spices. Cubes of paneer are added to this vibrant green sauce and simmered briefly. It’s a comforting and healthy dish, best served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 3 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons cream (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in hot water for 2 minutes, then transfer to cold water and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili; cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until soft.
  6. Pour in the spinach puree and mix well. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 3–4 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and add cream if using. Mix gently.
  9. Serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.
